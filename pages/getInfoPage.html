1. How to create a Samsung Smartthings token

Step 1. Access to https://account.smartthings.com/login and login SmartThings server.

Step 2. Access to https://account.smartthings.com/tokens and click “Generate new token”

Step 3. Select “Authorized Scopes” as Devices, InstalledApps, Location, Apps, Device Profiles, Schedules and Scenes as list and read options.

Step 4. After generate new token, copy token and paste.
